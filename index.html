Practice Makes Perfect

How Often You Should Practice

You should practice coding as often as possible. Practice just a few minutes each day.

Tips on Integrating Practice into Your Routine

There are several ways that you can make coding practice a part of your daily routine. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Set a specific time each day to practice.
  • Find a friend to practice with. You can hold each other accountable.
  • Teach someone else to code!
